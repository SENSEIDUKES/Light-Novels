## 2026-07-22 - Optimize Image History Filtering\n**Learning:** Found an anti-pattern where components conditionally filtered the global `imageHistory` array inside their render loops or `.map()` iterators (like in LivingCodexArtifacts, CharacterCard, LocationCard), which broke reference stability for children and forced O(N) redundant scans.\n**Action:** Replaced inline `.filter()` calls with `React.useMemo` to wrap derived entity arrays, and for large collections (artifacts), replaced nested iterations with a single memoized `Map` lookup to achieve O(N) performance and ensure stable component references.
